This Power BI dashboard provides a full analytics view of email marketing campaign performance, focusing on engagement, delivery efficiency, and audience behavior.
It enables marketers to monitor key metrics, compare campaign types, analyze audience segments, and identify opportunities to increase conversions.

The dashboard consists of two main pages:
Executive Summary
Audience Insights

ğŸ” Key Insights
The dashboard reveals valuable marketing insights, including:
Overall email send volume, delivery rate, and open rate trends
Click rate (CTR) and conversion performance across the quarter
Campaign-type comparisons: Promotional, Newsletter, Webinars, Lead Nurturing
Audience behavior by segment and device type
Engagement distribution across countries and regions
